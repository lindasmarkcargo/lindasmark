Good to Know
Important information.

A few things to keep in mind before you book.

Multiple packages can be consolidated into one shipment — great for families sending several items at once.
Oversized lightweight packages may be charged based on dimensional weight rather than actual weight.
Customs duties and taxes in Ghana are the responsibility of the recipient and are paid separately upon arrival.
Refrigerated or perishable shipments require a separate quote. Contact us directly to discuss.
