FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS.

Everything you need to know before you ship.

Getting Started
How do I book a shipment?

Fill out the booking form on our website or message us directly on WhatsApp at (571) 292-0125. WhatsApp is the fastest way to get a response — we typically reply within a few hours during business hours.

Do you have a minimum shipment size?

There's no strict minimum size — shipments up to 10kg are charged a flat $150 rate, so smaller packages are welcome too.

Where are you located?

We're based in Manassas Park, Virginia. You can ship directly to our address from any US retailer, or drop off your packages in person.

Pricing & Payment
How much does shipping cost?

Shipments up to 10kg are a flat $150. From 10.1kg to 12kg, it's a flat $180. Above 12kg, the rate is $15.99 per kg. Your final cost is based on the actual weight after we receive and weigh your packages — no hidden fees.

What if my package is large but light?

Oversized lightweight packages may be charged by dimensional weight rather than actual weight. We'll let you know upfront if this applies to your shipment.

When is payment due?

Payment is required before your shipment departs.

Are customs duties included in the price?

No — customs duties and taxes in Ghana are paid separately by the recipient upon arrival. We'll make sure your recipient knows what to expect.

Shipping & Delivery
How long does delivery take?

3–5 business days from the date your cargo departs. Many customers have received their packages even faster.

Can I send multiple packages together?

Yes — we consolidate multiple packages into one shipment. This is a great option for families sending several items at once, and it helps reduce your overall cost.

Do you ship refrigerated or perishable items?

These require a separate quote. Contact us directly to discuss your needs before booking.

Receiving in Ghana
How does pickup work in Accra?

We coordinate arrival and connect your recipient with our Accra warehouse for pickup. Local delivery options are also available — ask us about this when booking.

Will my recipient be notified when the package arrives?

Yes. We keep both you and your recipient updated at every stage — when cargo departs the US and when it's ready for pickup in Accra.

Business & Bulk Shipments
Do you handle bulk or business shipments?

Yes — we work with businesses and high-volume shippers regularly. Whether you're importing US goods for resale in Ghana or sending large recurring shipments, we can accommodate you. Contact us to discuss your volume and we'll build a plan around your needs.

Do you offer discounts for large shipments?

Yes. Volume discounts are available for bulk and business shipments. Reach out via WhatsApp or email to get a custom quote based on your shipment size and frequency.

Can you handle recurring business shipments?

Absolutely. If you ship regularly, we can set up a consistent schedule so your supply chain stays predictable. We're a reliable partner, not just a one-time service.
